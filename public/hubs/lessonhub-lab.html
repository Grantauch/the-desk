LessonHub Prototype Pack

Playable classroom ideas.

Four standalone demos showing what HTML LessonHubs can do when they act more like games than worksheets. Open them, click around, break them, and steal the mechanics for real units.

What this pack is testing

  • Game state, scoring, feedback, retries, and end screens without needing student accounts.
  • Drag and drop evidence sorting with a case strength report.
  • Branching decisions where student choices change meters and outcomes.
  • Simulation mechanics, random event cards, resources, and survival goals.
